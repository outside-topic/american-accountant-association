Academic Collaboration

Elevating Excellence Through Strategic Partnerships

The Institutional Affiliation Program of the American Association of Accountants (AAA) is designed to recognize, support, and collaborate with educational institutions, professional training providers, and corporate academies.

Through institutional affiliation, AAA establishes a structured partnership framework that enables organizations to align their academic programs, curriculum standards, and assessment methodologies with internationally benchmarked competencies.

"Affiliated institutions become part of a professional ecosystem committed to excellence, integrity, innovation, and lifelong learning."

Step-by-Step

The Affiliation Journey

AAA follows a structured, transparent, and quality-driven affiliation process to ensure mutual excellence.

Step 1: Expression of Interest (EOI)

Submission of organizational profile, program portfolio, and strategic objectives for initial review.

01
Step 2: Eligibility Screening

AAA reviews legal status, governance structure, academic credibility, and ethical compliance.

02
Step 3: Self-Assessment

Detailed documentation of curriculum alignment, faculty qualifications, and facilities.

03
Step 4: Evaluation & Review

Desktop review and virtual or onsite assessment including faculty and management interviews.

04
Step 5: Affiliation Decision

Final approval or improvement plan issued by the AAA Affiliation Committee.

05
Step 6: Agreement & Onboarding
Formal agreement covering:
  • Branding usage & Compliance
  • Reporting requirements
06
Step 7: Continuous Monitoring

Annual reporting, periodic audits, and continuous improvement feedback loops.
