To recognize senior leadership, strategic influence, and professional contribution.

Typical Profile

  • CFOs and finance directors
  • Senior partners and consultants
  • Controllers and senior managers
  • Academics and policy leaders
  • Entrepreneurs and advisors

Eligibility

  • Finance & Accounting education
  • Minimum senior-level experience
  • Leadership responsibility
  • Evidence of professional impact

Professional Contribution Expectation

Senior members are expected to contribute through mentoring, policy engagement, thought leadership, and professional development. This commitment ensures the growth and integrity of the global accounting profession.
